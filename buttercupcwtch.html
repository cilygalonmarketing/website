Buttercup Cwtch yw bwthyn delfrydol a hardd wedi'i lenwi gyda'r trysorau mor brydferth fe allech chi ffeindio. Gyda popeth wedi'i grefftio a llaw, rydych chi'n gwerthfawrogi pop manylion i'r cynnyrch. Mae'r stoc yn amrywio o cerdiau, atgelion, teganau plant a llawer mwy. A paid ag anghofio'r nifer o cynnyrchion Cymraeg yna, byddech chi'n siwr o dysgu gair newydd!

Buttercup Cwtch is a beautiful idyllic cottage filled with the prettiest treasures you could find. With all of their items hand-crafted, you appreciate every detail of their products. Their stock ranges from cards, accessories, ornaments, childrens toys and many more. Not to mention the incredible amount of welsh products here, you're sure to learn some new welsh words!
