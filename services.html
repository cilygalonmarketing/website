Cil-Y-Galon Marchnata Annibynnol yw cwmni marchnata yn De Cymru sy'n gweithio gyda busnesau annibynnol Stryd Mawr. Yn gwahanol i cwmnioedd eraill, dyn ni'n edrych at ein cleients fel ein gymdeithas. Gan gweithio gyda'r humneilltywr, rydyn ni'n gallu creu perthynas clos a ddibynadwy gyda'r cleients are safle un-i-un. Mae hyn yn gadael i ni ddeall beth mae pob cleient angen ac yn fwy bwysig, beth yw eu nod. Rydyn ni'n meddwl am ein cwmni-cleients fel un teulu mawr, achos cymuned sy'n bwysig i ni. Felly, rydyn ni'n rheoli cyfryngau cymdeithasol a'r gwefan gan ymgorffori i gyd o'n cleients. Ond, rydyn ni'n mwy na hapus i rhedeg rhai ein cleients hefyd, ac yn dylunio'i gwefanau, ymgyrchoedd hyrwyddo a hysbysebion i'r manylion olaf. Rydyn ni yma i rhedeg popeth tu allan o eich cwmni, felly yr unig peth sydd angen i ti becso am yw beth sy'n digwydd tu mean eich siop!
Cil-Y-Galon Independant Marketing is a South Wales based marekting company that works with independent High Street businesses. Unlike other businesses, we don't look at our clients as just 'clients', as they are our community. Working with independents, we're able to establish a close, trustwrothy relationship with our clients on a one-to-one basis. This allows us to have a better understanding of what each client needs and most importantly, what their goal is. We treat our company-client group as one large family, as community is what's important to us. And so, we manage social medias and the website by incorporating all our clients. However, we are more than happy running our clients social medias and designing their websites, promotional campaigns and advertisements down to the very last detail. We are here to run everything that happens outside of your businesses, so that all you have to worry about is what's happening inside your shop!
